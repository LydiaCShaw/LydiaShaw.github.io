Bus Reservation System Based on Smartphone Mobile Terminal

  • Project Background

    Bus Reservation System was not only DCL project but also SIVI competition in 2017.

    During the construction of the new campus, professors and supervisors have to travel from the old and new campuses for attending classes or inspections. In 2016, the number of students on the Hunnan campus of Northeastern University increased to 10,000.However, faculties moved from Nanhu campus still surged.

    Due to the close interaction between the two campuses, students and teachers going back and forth was considered rapidly. The number of school buses fluctuates greatly in different time periods and on different days, it is difficult to predict the actual number of school buses. This results in the phenomenon that teachers and students cannot get on the bus during high peak hours.For example, faculty members in the college will be more than usual when meeting on Wednesday.

    On the contrary, the number of school buses is small or empty during low peak hours, apparently, at night or weekend, the number of teachers who need a bus is lower than working hours.

    Considering the wasting of rental funds, a bus (more than 60 seats) consumes 700 yuan/trip, if it only takes 1-2 people, a small passenger car with less rent will be cheaper and enough to meet the requirements.

    Therefore, to sum up, the following problems may currently exist:

    (1) The bus driver drove ahead of time, causing the staff miss the ride at the intermediate stop;

    (2) During high peak hours, buses arranged cannot meet the needs of personnel;

    (3) During low peak hours, excess buses causing rent waste;

    (4) Due to poor information, the faculty at the stopover site did not know whether the bus was full causing inconvenience to students and teachers;

    (5) In extreme weather, it is difficult to wait for the bus outside. It is important to know the exact arrival time of the bus.
