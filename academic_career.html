Biography

SHANG Linjing was born in Liaoning which is located in China.In 2019, Shang graduated from Northeastern University(NEU) and got the bachelor of engineering in biomedical engineering.Afterwords, she got the master of science in Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU).

Research Interests

  • Scoliosis
  • Spinal orthotics
  • Prevention of fragility fractures
  • Gait and Posture analysis
  • CAD/CAM in prosthetics and orthotics
  • Prosthetics and orthotics outcome evaluation

Education and Academic Qualifications

  • Doctor of Philosophy, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University
  • Master of Philosophy, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University
  • Postgraduate Diploma in Biomechanics, University of Strathclyde
  • Diploma in Mechanical Engineering, Vocational Training Council (Kwun Tong Technical Institute)
  • DipBIST, The University of Hong Kong
